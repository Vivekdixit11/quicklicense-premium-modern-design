Understanding EPR Annual Returns

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) is a mandatory environmental policy approach where producers take responsibility for the end-of-life management of their products. Annual returns are critical compliance documents that must be submitted to the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB).

Who Must File EPR Returns?

  • Producers: Companies manufacturing plastic packaging, electronics, or batteries
  • Importers: Businesses importing packaged goods, electronic equipment, or batteries into India
  • Brand Owners: Companies whose brand appears on plastic packaging, e-waste, or battery products
  • Recyclers: Entities involved in recycling and processing waste materials

Filing Deadlines & Penalties

Annual Return Deadline: Must be filed within the prescribed timeline as per CPCB notifications
